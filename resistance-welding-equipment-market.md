<p><p>Resistance welding equipment is used for joining two or more metal components together using the principle of resistance heating. This process involves passing an electric current through the metal components to be joined, generating heat at the point of contact. The heat melts the metal surfaces, creating a weld when pressure is applied. Resistance welding equipment is widely used in various industries like automotive, aerospace, electrical and electronics, construction, and others.</p><p>The resistance welding equipment market is experiencing steady growth due to the increasing demand for efficient and reliable welding methods in various industries.
