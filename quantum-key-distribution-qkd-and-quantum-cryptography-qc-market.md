<p><p>Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) is a technique that uses quantum mechanical principles to securely distribute cryptographic keys. It offers an unbreakable method of key distribution, as any attempt to intercept the quantum state would disturb it, alerting the users.</p><p>Quantum Cryptography (QC), on the other hand, is a broader field that encompasses various cryptographic protocols that utilize principles of quantum mechanics. These protocols ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and authenticity of data transmission. Besides QKD, QC also includes quantum-resistant algorithms and techniques, quantum random number generators, and quantum digital signatures.</p><p>The Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) and Quantum Cryptography (QC) Market are expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
