<p><strong>The Time of Flight (ToF) Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct ToF Sensors</li><li>Indirect ToF Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Time of Flight (ToF) sensors are used to measure the distance between an object and the sensor by calculating the time taken for light or sound waves to travel and reflect back. Direct ToF sensors detect distance directly through sensors that emit and receive light or sound waves. Indirect ToF sensors use additional components like lenses or mirrors to detect distance by analyzing the change in phase or frequency of the reflected waves. These sensors find applications in various industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and robotics, enabling accurate distance and proximity sensing.</p></p>
