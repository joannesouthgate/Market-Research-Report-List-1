<p><p>Blood purification equipment refers to medical devices used to remove waste products and toxins from the blood in patients with kidney dysfunction or other related conditions. Continuous Blood Purification (CBP) is a technique that involves a continuous process of blood purification, providing sustained therapy. Hemodialysis (HD) is a commonly used method that works by passing the patient's blood through an artificial kidney machine for filtration and purification. The "Others" market includes alternative blood purification methods such as hemodiafiltration, plasma exchange, and adsorption methods which are used depending on the patient's specific needs or medical conditions.</p></p>
