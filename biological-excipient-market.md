<p><p>Biological excipient market types can be categorized as oral and topical. Oral excipients are used in the formulation of tablets, capsules, and syrups to enhance drug delivery, stability, and taste. They can include binders, fillers, and disintegrants. Topical excipients are employed in creams, ointments, and gels to improve skin penetration, stability, and texture. These can include emulsifiers, solvents, and preservatives. Both oral and topical excipients play a crucial role in pharmaceutical formulations by ensuring optimal drug performance and patient acceptability.</p></p>
