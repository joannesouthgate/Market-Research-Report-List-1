<p><p>The flat-rolled stainless steel market consists of various types, including hot-rolled coil, hot-rolled strips, hot-rolled sheets, and plates. Hot-rolled coil is a flat product obtained from a steel slab that is heated and passed through a series of rollers. Hot-rolled strips are narrow pieces of steel also processed through high-temperature rollers. Hot-rolled sheets are larger flat pieces produced similarly to coils. Plates are flat, solid steel sheets rolled directly from a slab. Each product serves different applications in industries such as construction, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
