<p><p>Sugar centrifugal screens are used in the sugar industry to separate sugar crystals from the molasses produced during the extraction process. These screens operate on the principle of centrifugal force, as the rotating basket throws the sugar crystals against the screen, separating them from the molasses. The screens are made of stainless steel and have varying apertures, allowing for different grades of sugar to be produced.</p><p>The sugar centrifugal screens market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. One of the major factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for sugar worldwide. As the global population continues to grow, the demand for sugar as a sweetener and food additive is also increasing. Furthermore, the changing consumer preferences for natural and organic sweeteners are also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>In addition, the advancements in screen technology, such as the development of screens with improved durability and efficiency, are fueling the market growth.
