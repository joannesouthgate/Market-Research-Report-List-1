<p><p>The Ink Code Marking Machine market can be divided into two main types: contact and non-contact machines. Contact Ink Code Marking Machines are designed to come into direct contact with the surface being marked, typically using a rubber stamp or similar method. On the other hand, non-contact machines utilize technologies like inkjet or laser to mark surfaces without physical contact. Both types have their advantages and are used in various industries for applications such as product labeling, traceability, and expiration date coding.</p></p>
