<p><p>Pneumatically actuated ball valves are available in different market types based on the type of connection. Threaded connections refer to valves that can be screwed onto pipelines. Flanged connections involve valves that are bolted onto pipelines using flanges. Butt weld connections involve welding the valves directly onto the pipelines. Socket weld connections involve welding the valves onto pipelines using socket welds. These different types of connections provide options for the installation and connection of pneumatic ball valves based on specific project requirements.</p></p>
