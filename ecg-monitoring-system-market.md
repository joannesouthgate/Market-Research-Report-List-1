<p><strong>The ECG Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resting ECG</li><li>Stress ECG</li><li>Holter Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ECG monitoring system market comprises three types: resting ECG, stress ECG, and Holter Monitors. Resting ECG records the heart's electrical activity while the patient remains still. Stress ECG evaluates the heart's performance during physical exercise or stress conditions. Holter Monitors are portable devices that record the heart's electrical activity continuously over a prolonged period, usually 24 to 48 hours. These types help diagnose heart conditions, such as arrhythmias or ischemia, and provide valuable insights into a patient's cardiovascular health.</p></p>
