<p><ul><li>Stand-alone Humidifier</li><li>Integrated Humidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The active humidifier market consists of two main types: stand-alone humidifiers and integrated humidifiers. Stand-alone humidifiers are separate units that are placed in a specific area and perform the task of adding moisture to the air independently. On the other hand, integrated humidifiers are built-in components of a larger system, such as heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems. Integrated humidifiers work together with the system to maintain the desired humidity levels throughout a building or space.</p></p>
