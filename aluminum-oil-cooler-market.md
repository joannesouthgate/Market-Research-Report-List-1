<p><p>The aluminum oil cooler market consists of two main types: single pass and dual pass. In the single pass design, the coolant flows through the cooler only once, making it a simpler and more straightforward system. On the other hand, the dual pass design offers improved cooling efficiency by allowing the coolant to pass through the cooler twice before returning to the engine. This allows for better heat dissipation and can be especially beneficial in high-performance and heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
