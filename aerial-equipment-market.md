Self-propelled aerial equipment includes platforms, lifts, or scissor lifts that can move independently without external support. Vehicle-mounted aerial equipment, as the name implies, is installed on vehicles like trucks or vans, allowing for mobility and easy transportation. These market segments provide various options for different applications, providing flexibility and efficiency in accessing higher areas.</p></p>
