<p><p>Meat speciation testing is a market that involves determining the species of meat in food products. There are various types of techniques used in this market, including PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction), ELISA (Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Assay), and Molecular Diagnostics. PCR is a method that amplifies specific regions of DNA to identify the species present. ELISA is an immunological technique that uses antibodies to detect and quantify substances, such as proteins, in a sample. Molecular diagnostics involve analyzing nucleic acids to detect genetic variations and species-specific markers. These techniques help ensure food authenticity and safety.</p></p>
