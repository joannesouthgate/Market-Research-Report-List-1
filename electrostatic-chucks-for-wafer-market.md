<p><strong>The Electrostatic Chucks for Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrostatic Chucks for wafer market consists of two main types - Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks. </p><p>Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks use the principle of electrostatic attraction to hold wafers in place during semiconductor manufacturing processes. They rely on the Coulomb force between the chuck and the wafer to create a strong grip.</p><p>On the other hand, Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks utilize a combination of electrostatic and electrodynamic forces to achieve stable wafer clamping. They offer enhanced performance and reliability compared to Coulomb Type Chucks.</p></p>
