Fluorescent lighting, like T5 and compact fluorescent lights, offer a more energy-efficient option for smaller grow spaces. LED lights are quickly gaining popularity for their energy efficiency, long lifespan, and customizable spectrum options. Each type of grow light caters to different plant growth needs and space requirements in the market.</p></p>
