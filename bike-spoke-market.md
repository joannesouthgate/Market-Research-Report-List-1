<p><p>In the bike spoke market, there are various types of spokes available. Straight spokes are simple and commonly used, while single and double butt spokes have a thicker section at one or both ends, providing increased strength. Aviation blade spokes, as the name suggests, are designed like airplane wing blades, offering high aerodynamic efficiency. Straight-pull spokes have no bends and are pulled straight into the hub, resulting in greater stiffness and strength. These different spoke types cater to various cycling disciplines and user preferences, providing options for improved performance and functionality.</p></p>
