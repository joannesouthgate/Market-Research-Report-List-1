<p><p>Pedicle screw systems are used in spinal surgeries for stabilization and fusion. The market offers different types of pedicle screws, including conical pedicle screws, cylindrical pedicle screws, and dual-cored pedicle screws. Conical pedicle screws have a tapered design, allowing for easy insertion and reduced risk of pull-out. Cylindrical pedicle screws have a uniform diameter along their entire length, providing stability. Dual-cored pedicle screws have two cores, allowing for enhanced strength and stability. These different types cater to the specific needs and preferences of surgeons and patients in spinal surgeries.</p></p>
