<p><p>The Aircraft Engine Accessories Market is segmented into various types, including Voltage Regulators, Starters, Alternators, and Others. Voltage regulators regulate and stabilize the electrical current flow within the aircraft engine, ensuring consistent power supply. Starters are responsible for igniting the engine and starting its rotation. Alternators generate electrical power while the engine is running, recharging the aircraft's battery. The "Others" category encompasses additional accessories that play a crucial role in the engine's functioning, such as fuel pumps, oil coolers, and engine control systems.</p></p>
