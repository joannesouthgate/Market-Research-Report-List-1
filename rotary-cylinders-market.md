<p><p>The rotary cylinders market comprises three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric rotary cylinders. Pneumatic rotary cylinders operate using compressed air, making them suitable for applications that require swift motion and moderate force. Hydraulic rotary cylinders use hydraulic fluid to generate power, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications requiring high force and precise control. Electric rotary cylinders utilize an electric motor to produce motion, offering efficient and accurate movements for various applications. These different types cater to different needs and requirements within the market.</p></p>
