<p><p>Coconut extracts come in two main types: liquid and powder. Liquid extracts are obtained by solvents or pressing of coconut pulp, resulting in a concentrated form that retains the natural flavor and aroma. These extracts are commonly used in beverages, baked goods, and confectionery products. On the other hand, coconut powder extracts are made by drying the liquid extract, resulting in a fine powder that is easier to store and transport. Powder extracts are widely used in the food industry, particularly in the production of health supplements, protein bars, and dairy alternatives.</p></p>
