<p><p>Anti-Adhesion Products are used in surgeries to prevent the formation of scar tissue between internal organs. The main market types for these products include Seprafilm, Surgiwrap, and Other products. Seprafilm is a bioresorbable membrane that adheres to tissue surfaces, Surgiwrap is a polymeric film that creates a physical barrier between tissues, and other products include gel-based adhesion barriers. These products aim to reduce the risk of post-operative adhesions, helping to improve patient outcomes and reduce the need for additional surgeries.</p></p>
