<p><p>Single seat rowboats can be classified into two main market types: sculling rowboats and sweep rowboats. Sculling rowboats are designed with two oars, one in each hand, allowing the rower to propel the boat forward. On the other hand, sweep rowboats are equipped with a single oar that is operated with both hands, requiring coordinated movements from a rower. These market types cater to different preferences and rowing techniques, providing options for individuals who enjoy rowing independently or prefer a more coordinated rowing experience.</p></p>
