<p><p>Teledentistry refers to the provision of dental care and services remotely, through the use of telecommunication technology. The market can be categorized into three types based on the mode of interaction: synchronous, asynchronous, and others. Synchronous teledentistry involves real-time communication between dental professionals and patients, such as video conferencing. Asynchronous teledentistry allows patients to submit information and images to dentists, who review it later. The "others" category includes emerging technologies like remote monitoring devices or Artificial Intelligence that assist in dental diagnostics or treatment planning. These types enable greater access and convenience for patients while improving the efficiency of dental care.</p></p>
