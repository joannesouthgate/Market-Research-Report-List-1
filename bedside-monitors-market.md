<p><p>Bedside monitors are medical devices used to track vital signs and monitor patients' conditions. The market can be divided into three main types: high-acuity monitors, mid-acuity monitors, and low-acuity monitors. High-acuity monitors are designed for critical care units and intensive care settings, providing comprehensive data on vital signs and offering advanced monitoring capabilities. Mid-acuity monitors are suitable for step-down units and general wards, providing essential monitoring functions. Low-acuity monitors are designed for non-critical care areas, offering basic vital sign monitoring and alarms. Each type caters to different levels of patient acuity and healthcare settings.</p></p>
