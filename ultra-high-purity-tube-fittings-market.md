<p><p>The Ultra-High-Purity (UHP) Tube Fittings market consists of two major types: UHP Stainless Steel Tube Fittings and UHP PFA Tube Fittings. They are commonly used in applications where high purity and resistance to chemical reactions are required. On the other hand, UHP PFA Tube Fittings are manufactured using perfluoroalkoxy (PFA), a type of fluoropolymer. These fittings exhibit excellent chemical resistance and are often used in semiconductor, pharmaceutical, and biotechnology industries.</p></p>
