The machine works by injecting nitrogen gas into the packaging before sealing it, creating a nitrogen-rich environment that reduces product degradation due to oxygen exposure.</p><p>The Nitrogen Flushing Machine Market is experiencing steady growth due to several factors. <p><ul><li>Vertical Nitrogen Flushing Machine</li><li>Horizontal Nitrogen Flushing Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nitrogen Flushing Machine market is categorized into two main types: Vertical Nitrogen Flushing Machine and Horizontal Nitrogen Flushing Machine. Vertical machines are designed to flush nitrogen into vertically oriented containers, such as bottles or cans, to remove oxygen and extend product shelf life. On the other hand, Horizontal machines are used for horizontally oriented containers, like pouches or bags. These machines generate a controlled nitrogen atmosphere within the packaging, preserving the quality and freshness of the products inside.</p></p>
