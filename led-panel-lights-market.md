<p><p>LED panel lights are available in three different market types - embedded type, suspended type, and ceiling type. Embedded type panels are designed to be recessed into the ceiling, providing a seamless and clean look. Suspended type panels are suspended from the ceiling using wires or chains, allowing for adjustable height and flexibility in installation. Ceiling type panels are surface-mounted directly on the ceiling, making them ideal for spaces with low ceiling height or where recessed installation is not possible. These different market types cater to different installation requirements and offer versatility in the use of LED panel lights.</p></p>
