<p><p>The carotenoids market consists of different types such as astaxanthin, beta-carotene, canthaxanthin, lycopene, and lutein. Astaxanthin is a red pigment found in seafood, used in supplements for its antioxidant properties. Beta-carotene is an orange pigment found in fruits and vegetables like carrots, known for converting into vitamin A. Canthaxanthin is a dye used in the food industry and as a color additive in animal feed. Lycopene is a red pigment found in tomatoes and other fruits, popular for its antioxidant properties. Lutein is a yellow pigment found in leafy greens and eggs, known for promoting eye health.</p></p>
