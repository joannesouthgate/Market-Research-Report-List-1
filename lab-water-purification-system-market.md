<p><p>The Lab Water Purification System market consists of two main types: Point of Use Systems and Large Central Systems. Point of Use Systems are compact and installed directly in the laboratory, providing purified water to a specific location or equipment. This type is favored for its convenience and cost-effectiveness for smaller labs. On the other hand, Large Central Systems are designed for labs with higher water demands. These systems use a central purification unit to distribute purified water throughout the lab via a network of pipelines, serving multiple locations simultaneously.</p></p>
