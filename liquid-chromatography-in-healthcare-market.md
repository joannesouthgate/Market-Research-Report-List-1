<p><p>Liquid chromatography in healthcare market types include ion-exchange chromatography, partition chromatography, size-exclusion chromatography, and supercritical fluid chromatography. Ion-exchange chromatography separates molecules based on their charge, while partition chromatography separates them based on their polarity. Size-exclusion chromatography separates molecules according to their size, and supercritical fluid chromatography uses a supercritical fluid as the mobile phase. These techniques are widely used in healthcare for various applications, such as pharmaceutical analysis, protein purification, and drug discovery, due to their effectiveness in separating and analyzing complex mixtures of molecules.</p></p>
