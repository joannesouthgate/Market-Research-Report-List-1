<p><p>The static materials testing machines market includes two main types of machines: electromechanical and hydraulic testing machines. Electromechanical testing machines use electric motors to apply force and measure the deformation of materials, making them suitable for performing tests that require high speed and accuracy. Hydraulic testing machines, on the other hand, utilize hydraulic systems to generate force and measure the response of materials. These machines are more suitable for tests that involve higher loads and longer durations. Both types serve different purposes in the static materials testing market.</p></p>
