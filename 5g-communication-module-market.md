<p><p>The 5G Communication Module market can be categorized into three types. Firstly, the 5G Data Transmission Module enables high-speed data transfer in 5G networks, facilitating faster internet browsing and smoother streaming. Secondly, the 5G Transceiver Module acts as a transmitter and receiver, allowing devices to connect and communicate over 5G networks. Lastly, the ASK Superheterodyne Module employs Amplitude Shift Keying (ASK) modulation to enable wireless data transmission in 5G networks, ensuring efficient and reliable communication. These modules collectively contribute to the advancement and widespread adoption of 5G technology.</p></p>
