<p><p>Nonwoven materials and products market is divided into three main types based on the materials used: polypropylene, polyester, and nylon. Polypropylene nonwovens are known for their high strength and durability, making them ideal for applications such as geotextiles and automotive upholstery. Polyester nonwovens are valued for their versatility and resistance to chemicals and mildew, commonly used in healthcare and filtration applications. Nylon nonwovens offer superior abrasion resistance and moisture-wicking properties, making them suitable for protective apparel and industrial uses.</p></p>
