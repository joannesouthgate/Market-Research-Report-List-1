<p><strong>The Portable Electronic Blood Glucose Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Type</li><li>Electrode Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable electronic blood glucose meter market consists of two types: photoelectric type and electrode type. The photoelectric type uses a light source and a sensor to measure the glucose level in the blood. It works by shining a beam of light through a blood sample and measuring the amount of light absorbed. On the other hand, the electrode type uses electrodes to measure the electrical current generated by the glucose in the blood sample. Both types provide convenient and accurate glucose level readings for individuals with diabetes.</p></p>
