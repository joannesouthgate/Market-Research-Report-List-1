<p><p>The marine drone market consists of two main types: tele-operated marine drones and autonomous marine drones. Tele-operated marine drones are remotely controlled by human operators and rely on real-time communication to carry out specific tasks underwater. On the other hand, autonomous marine drones operate independently using pre-programmed instructions or artificial intelligence algorithms to navigate, collect data, and perform various underwater missions. Both types have unique applications and are continuously being developed and improved to meet the demands of marine exploration, research, surveillance, and other marine-related activities.</p></p>
