<p><p>Oil and gas logistics market types can be categorized into three main areas: upstream, midstream, and downstream. Upstream logistics involves activities related to exploration, production, and initial processing of oil and gas resources. Midstream logistics focuses on the transportation, storage, and distribution of crude oil and natural gas to refineries and processing facilities. Downstream logistics involves refining, marketing, and delivering the refined products to end-users such as refineries, gas stations, and industrial consumers. These segments of the oil and gas supply chain collectively support the exploration, production, and consumption of oil and gas resources.</p></p>
