<p><p>Zirconium oxide flap discs are abrasive tools used for grinding, polishing, and finishing various materials such as metal, wood, and plastics. These discs consist of a circular base plate made of fiber glass or plastic, with a coating of zirconium oxide abrasive grains on the surface. The abrasive grains are attached to the base plate using adhesives or by direct bonding. Zirconium oxide flap discs are known for their durability, high material removal rate, and ability to handle high-pressure applications.</p><p>The market for zirconium oxide flap discs is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the rising demand for abrasive tools in various industries such as automotive, metal fabrication, and construction.
