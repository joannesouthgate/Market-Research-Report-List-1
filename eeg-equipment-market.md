<p><strong>The EEG Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional EEG</li><li>Video EEG</li><li>Dynamic EEG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EEG equipment market is divided into three types: Conventional EEG, Video EEG, and Dynamic EEG. Conventional EEG refers to the traditional method of recording and analyzing brain activity using electrodes placed on the scalp. Video EEG combines video monitoring with EEG to observe and analyze brain activity during seizure events. Dynamic EEG involves continuous monitoring of brain activity over an extended period to capture and analyze long-term changes. These different types of EEG equipment cater to varied needs in neurology and offer advanced features for comprehensive brain analysis and diagnosis.</p></p>
