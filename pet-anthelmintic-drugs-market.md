<p><p>The pet anthelmintic drugs market can be classified into four types based on their intended use and administration method. External anthelmintics are used to treat external parasites such as ticks and fleas. Internal anthelmintics are given orally or through the pet's food to treat internal parasites like roundworms and tapeworms. Composite anthelmintics are a combination of external and internal treatments, targeting both external and internal parasites. Injection anthelmintics are administered by injection and are typically used to treat severe infestations or when oral administration is not possible.</p></p>
