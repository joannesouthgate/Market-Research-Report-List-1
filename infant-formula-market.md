<p><p>The infant formula market comprises various types of products tailored to meet the nutritional needs of babies at different stages. Starting milk formula, also known as first milk or stage 1 formula, is designed for infants from birth until six months of age. Follow-on milk formula, or stage 2 formula, is suitable for babies aged six months and older as a transition from breast milk or starting formula. Toddlers milk formula, or stage 3 formula, caters to the nutritional requirements of toddlers between the ages of one and three. Lastly, special milk formula is designed for infants with specific dietary needs, such as allergies or digestion issues.</p></p>
