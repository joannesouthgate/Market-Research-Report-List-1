<p><p>The Rigid Overhead Conductor-rail System (ROCS) is used to supply electrical power to moving machinery in industrial settings. There are two market types of ROCS: Hinged Type and Sliding Type. </p><p>Hinged Type market refers to a system where the conductor-rails are hinged at regular intervals, allowing them to swing freely when a moving machine changes direction. This ensures constant electrical connection throughout the movement.</p><p>On the other hand, Sliding Type market involves conductor-rails that are fixed and allow sliding contact shoes to make electrical connections. These systems are typically used in linear or circular layouts where machines move along a predetermined path.</p><p>Both types of ROCS market offer reliable and continuous power supply while accommodating the movement of machinery in industrial environments.</p></p>
