<p><p>The electric heater market encompasses various types of heaters. Immersion heaters are designed to heat liquids, while tubular heaters are versatile and commonly used for heating gases and solids. Circulation heaters are specifically designed for the circulation of fluid. Band heaters wrap around pipes and cylindrical objects to provide heat. Strip heaters are flat and flexible, often used for surface heating. Coil heaters have a coiled shape for efficient heating. Flexible heaters are thin, lightweight, and can be molded to fit various shapes. Lastly, the "Others" category includes various specialized electric heaters catering to specific needs.</p></p>
