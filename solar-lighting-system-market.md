<p><p>A solar lighting system is a form of lighting that utilizes solar energy to produce light. It consists of a solar panel, battery, charge controller, LED light source, and other necessary components. The charge controller regulates the flow of electricity, ensuring optimal charging and discharging of the battery. <p><p>The solar lighting system market can be categorized into types such as off-grid and hybrid systems. Off-grid solar lighting systems are independent of the main power grid and rely solely on solar energy for operation. These systems are commonly used in remote areas where electrical infrastructure is minimal or nonexistent. On the other hand, hybrid solar lighting systems combine solar energy with other sources such as wind or grid electricity to provide continuous illumination. This type of system is often used in areas with fluctuating weather conditions or unstable power supply.</p></p>
