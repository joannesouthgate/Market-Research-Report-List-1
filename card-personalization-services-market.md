<p><p>Card personalization services refer to the customization of cards, such as identification cards or credit cards, according to individual preferences or requirements. There are three main types of markets within this industry: dye sublimation, retransfer, and inkjet. Dye sublimation involves transferring dye onto the card through heat, resulting in vibrant and high-quality images. Retransfer uses a two-step printing process to transfer ink from a ribbon onto the card's surface, offering durability and increased security. Inkjet printing involves the direct application of ink onto the card, allowing for quick and cost-effective personalization.</p></p>
