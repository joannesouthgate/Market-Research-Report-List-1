<p><p>A standard spirit level, also known as a bubble level or simply a level, is a tool used to determine whether a surface is horizontal (level) or vertical (plumb). It consists of a rectangular or circular body containing a curved glass vial, filled with liquid and an air bubble. When the bubble is centered between two reference lines inside the vial, it indicates that the surface is level or plumb.</p><p>The standard spirit level market is expected to experience significant growth in the forecast period.
