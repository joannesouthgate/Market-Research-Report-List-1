<p><p>Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) Data Analysis Market Types refer to different technologies used in analyzing genetic information from DNA or RNA samples. Sequencing By Synthesis (SBS) Technology enables sequencing by detecting the addition of nucleotides during synthesis. Single Molecule Real-Time Sequencing (SMRT) Technology observes the real-time synthesis of DNA molecules. Ion Semiconductor Sequencing Technology uses semiconductor-based ion detection for sequencing. Other technologies in this market include nanopore sequencing and pyrosequencing. These technologies play a crucial role in accurately and efficiently deciphering genetic information for various applications in research, diagnostics, and personalized medicine.</p></p>
