<p><strong>The Combination Starters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Voltage Non Reversing</li><li>Full-Voltage Reversing</li><li>Multi-Speed</li><li>Reduced Voltage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Combination starters are used to control and protect electric motors. The full-voltage non-reversing starters are designed to start and stop the motor in a single direction. The full-voltage reversing starters can change the direction of the motor's rotation. Multi-speed starters allow for the adjustment of motor speed by switching between different sets of windings. Reduced voltage starters help reduce the high inrush current at motor startup, thereby minimizing electrical disturbances. These different market types cater to specific motor control and protection needs in various industrial applications.</p></p>
