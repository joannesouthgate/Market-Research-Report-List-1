<p><p>The medical image processing market consists of various types of imaging technologies such as CT-Scan, X-ray, ultrasound, MRI, nuclear imaging, and others. CT-Scan involves the use of X-rays and computer algorithms to create cross-sectional images of the body. X-ray technology utilizes electromagnetic radiation to produce images of the internal structures. Ultrasound employs high-frequency sound waves to generate images of organs and tissues. MRI utilizes magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images of the body. Nuclear imaging involves the administration of radioactive substances to visualize internal body functions. Lastly, "others" encompass additional imaging techniques including PET scan, mammography, and fluoroscopy, among others.</p></p>
