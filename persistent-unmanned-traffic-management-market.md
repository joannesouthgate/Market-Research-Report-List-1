<p><p>The Persistent Unmanned Traffic Management (UTM) market consists of three main types of infrastructure: Communication Infrastructure, Navigation Infrastructure, and Surveillance Infrastructure. </p><p>Communication Infrastructure includes the systems and technologies that enable reliable and efficient communication between unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) and ground control stations. This infrastructure ensures seamless connectivity and exchange of critical information for safe and effective UAS operations.</p><p>Navigation Infrastructure focuses on providing precise positioning, navigation, and timing (PNT) services to UAS. This infrastructure includes technologies like satellite-based navigation systems (e.g., GPS), ground-based navigation aids, and geofencing capabilities to enable accurate and reliable navigation for UAS.</p><p>Surveillance Infrastructure pertains to the systems and tools used to monitor and track UAS operations. This infrastructure includes surveillance sensors, radars, drones, and other technologies that provide situational awareness to UTM operators. This enables the detection of unauthorized or potentially hazardous UAS activities and ensures proper management and control of airspace.</p></p>
