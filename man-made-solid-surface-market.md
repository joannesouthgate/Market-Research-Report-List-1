<p><p>The man-made solid surface market can be categorized into three types: Alumina Trihydrate (ATH), Acrylic, and Others. Alumina Trihydrate (ATH) is a flame-retardant material commonly used in solid surfaces due to its excellent fire resistance properties. Acrylic, on the other hand, is known for its durability and versatility, making it a popular choice in the market. Lastly, the "Others" category includes different materials such as polyester, quartz, and epoxy, which offer unique features and characteristics to cater to specific needs in the solid surface industry.</p></p>
