<p><p>The erbium-doped laser gain media market consists of two main types: erbium-doped glasses and erbium-doped crystals. Erbium-doped glasses are made by adding erbium ions to a glass matrix. They are highly efficient and offer a broad emission bandwidth. On the other hand, erbium-doped crystals contain erbium ions incorporated into a crystalline structure. They provide a narrower emission bandwidth and higher power handling capabilities compared to glasses. Both types are widely used in various applications such as telecommunications, material processing, and medical surgeries for their unique properties and performance.</p></p>
