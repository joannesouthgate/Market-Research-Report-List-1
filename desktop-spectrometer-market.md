<p><p>The Desktop Spectrometer market can be categorized into two types: Optical Absorption Spectrometer and Optical Emission Spectrometer. An Optical Absorption Spectrometer analyzes the absorption of light by a sample, allowing scientists to determine the concentration of specific compounds within the sample. On the other hand, an Optical Emission Spectrometer measures the intensity of light emitted by the sample, enabling the identification and quantification of different chemical elements present. Both types of spectrometers are beneficial tools used in various scientific research and analysis applications.</p></p>
