<p><p>Intelligent lighting controls market consists of two main types: wired technology and wireless technology. Wired technology refers to lighting systems that are connected through physical wires, allowing for seamless communication between the lighting fixtures and control devices. On the other hand, wireless technology refers to lighting systems that use wireless communication protocols, such as Wi-Fi or Bluetooth, to connect the lighting fixtures and control devices. This eliminates the need for physical wires, providing more flexibility in terms of installation and control. Both types offer various benefits and can be chosen based on the specific requirements of the user.</p></p>
