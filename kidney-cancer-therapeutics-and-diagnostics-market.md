<p><p>The kidney cancer therapeutics and diagnostics market includes various types of procedures to diagnose and treat kidney cancer. Cystoscopy involves inserting a thin tube with a camera into the bladder to examine the urinary tract. Biopsy involves removing a sample of kidney tissue for examination. Intravenous pyelogram is a procedure where a contrast dye is injected into a vein followed by X-ray imaging to visualize the kidneys. CT scan uses X-rays to create detailed images of the kidneys. Kidney ultrasounds use sound waves to create images of the kidneys. Other diagnostic methods may include blood tests or genetic testing.</p></p>
