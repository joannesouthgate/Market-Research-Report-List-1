<p><strong>The EEG Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional EEG</li><li>Video EEG</li><li>Dynamic EEG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EEG systems are used to measure and record the electrical activity of the brain. There are three main types of EEG systems in the market. Conventional EEGs are used to monitor brain activity in a clinical setting. Video EEGs combine conventional EEGs with video recording to capture and analyze both brain activity and physical movements. Dynamic EEGs use advanced algorithms and artificial intelligence to provide real-time analysis and detection of abnormal brain activity. Each type of EEG system has its own unique features and applications in diagnosing and managing neurological disorders.</p></p>
