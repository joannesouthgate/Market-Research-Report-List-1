<p><strong>The Light Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Light Sensor</li><li>Non-Contact Light Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Light Sensors market consists of two main types: Contact Light Sensors and Non-Contact Light Sensors. Contact Light Sensors need physical contact with the object to detect light, typically by using a photodiode or phototransistor. On the other hand, Non-Contact Light Sensors can detect light without any physical contact with the object, using technologies like infrared or laser. Both types are widely utilized in various industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation for applications like proximity sensing, ambient light detection, and object detection.</p></p>
