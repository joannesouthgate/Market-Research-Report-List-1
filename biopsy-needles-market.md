<p><p>The biopsy needles market can be categorized into two types, fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic biopsy needles offer a higher level of automation and are designed to perform the entire biopsy procedure with minimal user intervention. These needles have features like automated firing, sample collection, and ejection. On the other hand, semi-automatic biopsy needles require some user involvement during the procedure, such as manual firing or sample retrieval. Both types of needles are widely used in various medical settings for performing biopsies and extracting tissue samples for analysis and diagnosis.</p></p>
