<p><strong>The Flying Laser Markers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CO2 Laser Markers</li><li>Fiber Laser Markers</li><li>UV Laser Markers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flying laser markers market offers three main types of laser marking technologies: CO2 laser markers, fiber laser markers, and UV laser markers. CO2 laser markers use a carbon dioxide gas mixture to produce laser beams, making them suitable for different materials like plastics, wood, and glass. Fiber laser markers employ optical fibers to create the laser beam, allowing for higher precision and speed on materials like metal and plastics. UV laser markers operate using ultraviolet light, delivering a more precise and permanent marking on various surfaces, particularly heat-sensitive ones.</p></p>
