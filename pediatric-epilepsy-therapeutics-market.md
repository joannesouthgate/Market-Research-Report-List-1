<p><p>The Pediatric Epilepsy Therapeutics Market is segmented into various types based on the specific types of epilepsy that occur in children. Benign Rolandic Epilepsy is a common type that affects the face and mouth muscles. Childhood Absence Epilepsy is characterized by brief staring spells and interruptions in consciousness. Juvenile Myoclonic Epilepsy involves muscle jerks, usually in the arms and shoulders. Infantile Spasms cause sudden, brief, and frequent muscle contractions. Lennox-Gastaut Syndrome is a severe form of epilepsy that causes multiple seizure types and intellectual disabilities.</p></p>
