Mills perform tasks like cutting, drilling, and milling by removing material from workpieces using rotary cutters. Grinders, on the other hand, are used for precision grinding operations. The "others" category encompasses various CNC machines like routers, plasma cutters, and 3D printers that perform specific functions based on their design and purpose.</p></p>
