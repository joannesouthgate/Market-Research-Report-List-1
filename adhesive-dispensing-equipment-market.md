<p><p>The adhesive dispensing equipment market includes various types of dispensing systems. Continuous dispensing systems offer a continuous flow of adhesive without interruption. Power-driven systems use motorized mechanisms to dispense adhesives quickly and efficiently. Manual-driven systems require human effort to dispense adhesives. Foot control systems enable operators to control the dispensing process using their feet. Multiple dispensing systems allow for the simultaneous dispensing of multiple adhesives. Spray systems disperse the adhesive in a fine mist. Suck back control systems prevent the adhesive from dripping after dispensing. Programmable microprocessor systems provide precise control over the dispensing process.</p></p>
