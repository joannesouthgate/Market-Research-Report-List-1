<p><p>The circuit board terminal market is categorized into three types based on the American Wire Gauge (AWG) size of the wires connected to the terminals. Below 10 AWG refers to terminals designed for wires with a diameter smaller than or equal to 10 AWG. 10-20 AWG terminals are suitable for wires with a diameter between 10 AWG and 20 AWG. Above 20 AWG terminals are intended for wires with a diameter greater than 20 AWG. These categories help manufacturers in producing terminals specifically tailored to the wire size, ensuring optimal connectivity and performance.</p></p>
