<p><p>Digital blood pressure monitors come in two types: Detached Type and Integrated Type. Detached Type monitors consist of a cuff that needs to be wrapped around the upper arm and is connected via a tube to a separate display device. Integrated Type monitors, on the other hand, have the cuff and display device combined as one unit, offering ease of use and portability. Both types provide accurate and convenient hypertension monitoring at home, enabling individuals to keep track of their blood pressure levels regularly.</p></p>
