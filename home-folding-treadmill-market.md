<p><p>The home folding treadmill market consists of two main types: manual and electric treadmills. Manual treadmills operate solely based on the user's movement, requiring them to exert effort to propel the belt. They are usually more affordable and compact, making them suitable for small spaces. On the other hand, electric treadmills are powered by a motor and offer various features like adjustable speed and incline. They provide a more convenient and customizable workout experience but tend to be larger and more expensive. Both types cater to different preferences and budgets.</p></p>
