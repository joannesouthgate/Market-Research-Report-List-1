<p><p>The Helicoidal Impeller Pump market consists of three main types: Open Screw Impeller Pump, Closed Screw Impeller Pump, and Semi Open Screw Impeller Pump. </p><p>Open Screw Impeller Pump features an uncovered, helical-shaped impeller that allows for high efficiency and easy maintenance. </p><p>Closed Screw Impeller Pump, on the other hand, has a fully enclosed impeller, providing better safety and preventing clogs or leaks. </p><p>Semi-Open Screw Impeller Pump combines properties of both, featuring a partially covered impeller for efficient pumping while reducing the risk of clogging. These types cater to different needs within the market, offering various advantages based on their design and functionalities.</p></p>
