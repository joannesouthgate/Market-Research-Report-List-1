<p><p>The Pneumatic Ventilators market consists of two main types: Floor-standing and Portable. Floor-standing ventilators are designed to be securely positioned on the floor and are typically used in hospitals and healthcare facilities where there is a need for constant respiratory support to patients. Portable ventilators, on the other hand, are compact and lightweight devices that can be easily moved and transported. These are commonly used in emergency medical services or during patient transportation in ambulances. Both types of ventilators play a crucial role in providing mechanical ventilation to individuals with breathing difficulties.</p></p>
