<p><p>The oil burner market is divided into three types: light oil burner, heavy oil burner, and dual-use burner. A light oil burner is designed specifically for burning lighter oils, such as diesel or kerosene. A heavy oil burner, on the other hand, is designed to burn heavier oils, such as residual fuel oil. Lastly, a dual-use burner is capable of burning both light and heavy oils, providing flexibility to users based on their fuel availability and requirements. Each type caters to different fuel specifications, ensuring efficient and reliable combustion.</p></p>
