<p><p>Document creation software can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. In the cloud-based market, the software is accessed and operated through the internet, allowing users to create, edit, and share documents from anywhere with an internet connection. This offers flexibility and scalability, typically through a subscription-based model. On the other hand, in the on-premises market, the software is installed and operated locally on the user's hardware. This provides greater control over data security but may require additional resources for maintenance and updates.</p></p>
