<p><p>The telecommunications cable market comprises different types of cables including transmission lines, waveguides, and optical fibers. Transmission lines, such as coaxial cables, are used to transmit high-frequency electrical signals over long distances. Waveguides are used to guide electromagnetic waves, mostly in high-frequency applications. Lastly, optical fibers are thin, flexible strands of glass or plastic that use light to transmit data over long distances. They are highly efficient and capable of high data transmission rates, making them a popular choice for long-distance communication.</p></p>
