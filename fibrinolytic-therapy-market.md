Surgical treatment includes procedures like embolectomy, where clots are surgically removed from blood vessels. Other treatment methods include the use of mechanical devices, such as catheters, to break up and remove blood clots. These different types of treatments aim to restore blood flow by dissolving or removing clots, helping to prevent serious complications like heart attacks or strokes.</p></p>
