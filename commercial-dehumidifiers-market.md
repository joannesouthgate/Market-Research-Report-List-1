<p><p>Commercial dehumidifiers are classified into two main market types: portable dehumidifiers and whole-home dehumidifiers. Portable dehumidifiers are designed to be easily movable and are commonly used in small commercial spaces or specific areas where excess moisture needs to be removed. On the other hand, whole-home dehumidifiers are installed within the ventilation system of a building and are capable of regulating humidity levels in large commercial spaces or entire buildings. Both types serve the purpose of minimizing excess moisture, but differ in terms of mobility and capacity.</p></p>
