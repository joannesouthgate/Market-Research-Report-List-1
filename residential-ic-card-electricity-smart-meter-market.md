<p><p>The residential IC card electricity smart meter market is divided into two types: single phase and three phase. A single phase smart meter is used in households with a lower electricity demand. It measures the electricity consumption of a single circuit and is suitable for small homes or apartments. On the other hand, a three phase smart meter is used in households with higher electricity demand, such as larger homes or buildings. It measures the electricity consumption of three circuits and provides more accurate data for heavier electrical loads.</p></p>
