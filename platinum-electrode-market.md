<p><ul><li>Counter Electrode</li><li>Single Electrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platinum electrode market primarily consists of two types: Counter electrode and Single electrode. A counter electrode is an electrode placed opposite to the working electrode in an electrochemical cell to facilitate the flow of electrical current. It completes the electrical circuit and allows for the transfer of electrons. On the other hand, a single electrode refers to the use of only one platinum electrode in an electrochemical system. These different market types cater to distinct requirements and applications in various industries, such as electroplating, fuel cells, sensors, and analytical chemistry.</p></p>
