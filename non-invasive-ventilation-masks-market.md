<p><p>Non-invasive ventilation masks are medical devices used to deliver oxygen or air to patients without the need for invasive procedures, such as intubation. These masks are attached to a ventilator or a respiratory support system that helps deliver a controlled amount of air or oxygen to the patient's lungs. They are primarily used in conditions such as chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), sleep apnea, and acute respiratory distress syndrome (ARDS).</p><p>The non-invasive ventilation masks market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of respiratory diseases, such as asthma, COPD, and sleep apnea, is driving the demand for non-invasive ventilation masks. Additionally, advancements in mask designs and technology, leading to improved patient comfort and ease of use, are further fueling market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic has created a surge in demand for non-invasive ventilation masks as a supportive therapy for patients suffering from respiratory distress. The increasing number of COVID-19 cases worldwide has boosted the adoption of non-invasive ventilation techniques, propelling the market growth.</p><p>In terms of trends, manufacturers are focusing on developing masks with enhanced features such as adjustable straps, leak-proof design, and lightweight materials. Additionally, the introduction of wireless and wearable mask technology is anticipated to revolutionize the non-invasive ventilation market.</p><p>Overall, the non-invasive ventilation masks market is expected to witness substantial growth due to increasing respiratory disease prevalence, advancements in mask technology, and the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic.
