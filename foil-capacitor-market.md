<p><p>Foil capacitors are classified into three types: metal film, ceramic film, and polymer film. Metal film capacitors are made of a thin metal foil and are commonly used in applications requiring high-precision and stability. Ceramic film capacitors consist of a ceramic material with thin metal layers, offering good insulation and low cost. Polymer film capacitors are made of a polymer film and provide high capacitance and low leakage current. Each type caters to different requirements and conditions, making them suitable for diverse electronic applications.</p></p>
