<p><p>The Virtual Queue Management System market comprises different types such as structured queue, unstructured queue, kiosk-based queue, and moving queue. Structured queue systems allow customers to join a queue digitally, receive notifications, and wait for their turn. Unstructured queue systems are flexible and allow customers to move freely within the premises. Kiosk-based queues enable customers to register themselves using self-service kiosks. Moving queues are mobile systems where customers can join a queue remotely and receive updates about their position in the queue. These systems streamline customer flow and improve efficiency in various settings.</p></p>
