<p><p>The static cone penetrometer market includes hand-operated and self-start models. Hand-operated static cone penetrometers are manually controlled and commonly used in smaller projects or for limited soil testing. They require physical effort to penetrate the ground. On the other hand, self-start static cone penetrometers are automated and require less physical effort. They are suitable for larger projects and provide more accurate and efficient results. Both types of static cone penetrometers are used to measure the resistance and strength of soils during construction and geotechnical engineering projects.</p></p>
