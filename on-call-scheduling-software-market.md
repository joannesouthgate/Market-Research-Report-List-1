<p><p>On-Call Scheduling Software market has two major types: Cloud-Based and On-Premise. Cloud-Based software is hosted on remote servers, accessible via the internet, and managed by a third-party provider. It offers flexibility, scalability, and easy remote access for users. On the other hand, On-Premise software is installed locally, on the user's own servers, and managed by their IT department. It provides complete control, customization, and data security to the organization but requires maintenance and infrastructure investment. Both options cater to different needs, and the choice depends on the organization's priorities.</p></p>
