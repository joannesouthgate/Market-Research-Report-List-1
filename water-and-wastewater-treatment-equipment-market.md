<p><p>The water and wastewater treatment equipment market is divided into several types, including membrane, separation, disinfection, and others. Separation treatment involves techniques like filtration, sedimentation, and coagulation to remove suspended solids. Disinfection treatment uses chemical or physical processes to kill or deactivate microorganisms in water. The "others" category includes various technologies like adsorption, ion exchange, and oxidation for water purification. These types of equipment are essential for ensuring the safety and quality of water and wastewater before it is released back into the environment.</p></p>
