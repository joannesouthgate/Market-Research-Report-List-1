<p><p>A medical centrifuge is a laboratory instrument used in medical and research settings to separate substances of different densities in a sample. It works on the principle of centrifugal force, which is applied to the sample by spinning it at high speeds. This force causes the denser components to move towards the outer edges while the lighter components stay closer to the center.</p><p>The medical centrifuge market has been experiencing steady growth due to several factors. The increasing prevalence of chronic diseases is driving the demand for diagnostic tests, which require the use of medical centrifuges.
