<p><p>Dental materials refer to the substances used for the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of dental conditions and diseases. These materials are used in various dental procedures, including dental restoration, orthodontics, endodontics, periodontics, and prosthetics. They are designed to mimic the natural appearance and function of teeth, while also ensuring durability and biocompatibility.</p><p>The global dental material market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years. The market is primarily driven by factors such as the increasing prevalence of dental diseases and conditions, rising geriatric population, growing demand for cosmetic dentistry, advancements in dental materials, and the introduction of new dental treatment procedures. Additionally, the expanding dental tourism industry and the rising disposable income of individuals in emerging economies are further fueling the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing several trends that are expected to shape its future growth. One prominent trend is the increasing adoption of digital dentistry, which encompasses the use of computer-aided design and computer-aided manufacturing (CAD/CAM) technologies for dental procedures. Digital dentistry offers numerous benefits, including improved accuracy, enhanced patient comfort, and reduced treatment time.</p><p>Furthermore, there is a growing focus on the development of bioactive materials for dental applications. These materials have the ability to release beneficial ions, such as calcium and fluoride, which can promote tooth remineralization and prevent tooth decay. This trend aligns with the rising preference for minimally invasive dental procedures that aim to preserve natural tooth structure.</p><p>Overall, with the increasing demand for dental materials and the introduction of innovative technologies, the global dental material market is expected to witness significant growth. Composites consist of resin and are preferred for their tooth-colored appearance, making them ideal for dental restorations and repairs.
