<p><p>The Retail eCommerce Platform market can be categorized into two types: Cloud-Based and On-Premise. Cloud-Based platforms operate on remote servers, accessible through the internet. They offer scalability, flexibility, and affordability as businesses only pay for the resources they use. On-Premise platforms are installed and operated directly on a company's own servers.
