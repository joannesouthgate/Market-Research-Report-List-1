<p><p>Trade finance services refer to various financial instruments and support mechanisms that facilitate international trade transactions. Guarantees are commitments by a bank to pay a specified amount if the beneficiary fails to fulfill their obligations. Letters of credit are payment guarantees issued by banks on behalf of importers to ensure payment to exporters. Documentary collection involves banks handling shipping documents and payments between buyer and seller. Supply chain financing assists in optimizing cash flow throughout the supply chain. Factoring involves the sale of accounts receivable to a third party at a discount to obtain immediate cash.</p></p>
