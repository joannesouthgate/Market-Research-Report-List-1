<p><strong>The Dolby Atmos Soundbar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Upward-firing Soundbar</li><li>Virtualized Soundbar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dolby Atmos Soundbar market consists of two main types: Upward-firing Soundbars and Virtualized Soundbars. Upward-firing Soundbars feature additional upward-firing speakers that bounce sound off the ceiling, creating a three-dimensional audio experience. These soundbars are ideal for rooms with low ceilings or limited space for additional speakers. On the other hand, Virtualized Soundbars use advanced audio processing technology to simulate the immersive Dolby Atmos effect without the need for extra upward-firing speakers. Virtualized Soundbars offer a more convenient and cost-effective solution for those seeking an enhanced audio experience.</p></p>
