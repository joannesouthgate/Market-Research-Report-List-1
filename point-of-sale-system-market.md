<p><p>The Point of Sale (POS) system market consists of three main types: Desktop POS, Handheld POS, and Mobile POS. Desktop POS refers to traditional systems that are fixed in a physical location, such as a cash register at a store. Handheld POS involves portable devices, like tablets or smartphones, that allow employees to process transactions from anywhere within a store. Mobile POS, on the other hand, enables businesses to conduct transactions outside of a physical store, utilizing mobile devices and wireless connectivity for sales on-the-go.</p></p>
