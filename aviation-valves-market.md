<p><strong>The Aviation Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Poppet Valves</li><li>Pilot Valves</li><li>Flapper-nozzle Valves</li><li>Ball and Plug Valves</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation valves market includes various types of valves that are crucial for the efficient operation of aircraft. Poppet valves are commonly used and consist of a disk-like element that opens or closes a passage. Pilot valves control the flow of fluids by using a smaller valve to activate a larger one. Flapper-nozzle valves regulate the airflow by using a flapper to change the area of the nozzle opening. Ball and plug valves use a rotating ball or plug to control fluid flow. Other types of valves used in aviation include butterfly valves, gate valves, and check valves.</p></p>
