<p><ul><li>Centralized Type</li><li>Distributed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Central Vacuum Cleaner market consists of two main types: Centralized Type and Distributed Type. The Centralized Type involves a single large vacuum unit installed in a central location, connected to multiple ports placed throughout the facility. This type allows for efficient cleaning by eliminating the need to move the vacuum unit. On the other hand, the Distributed Type utilizes multiple smaller vacuum units placed in different areas of the facility. This type offers flexibility and convenience as each unit can be used independently, but may require more effort to move and maintain.</p></p>
