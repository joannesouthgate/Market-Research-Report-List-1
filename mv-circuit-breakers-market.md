<p><strong>The MV Circuit Breakers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB)</li><li>Moulded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB)</li><li>Air Circuit Breaker (ACB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MV circuit breakers market comprises three main types: Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB), Moulded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB), and Air Circuit Breaker (ACB). MCBs are commonly used for low voltage applications and provide protection against overload and short circuits. MCCBs are suitable for moderate to high voltage applications and offer thermal and magnetic trip mechanisms. ACBs are designed for high voltage applications and offer a wide range of functionalities, including remote control capabilities. Each type caters to specific voltage levels and offers different features for effective circuit protection.</p></p>
