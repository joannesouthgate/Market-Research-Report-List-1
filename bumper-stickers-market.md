<p><p>Bumper stickers can be categorized into two main market types: clear and opaque. These types of stickers are often preferred for their clean and minimalist look. On the other hand, opaque bumper stickers have a solid background that completely covers the surface they are displayed on. These stickers provide a more vibrant and eye-catching appearance, as the solid color background enhances the visibility of the design or message.</p></p>
