<p><p>Thermoforming Packaging Market offers different types of packaging solutions including Blister Packs, Clamshells, Vacuum Packs, and Skin Packs. Blister Packs are formed by sealing a product between a blister and a backing card. Clamshells are hinged containers that enclose a product safely. Vacuum Packs involve removing air from the package to create a vacuum seal. Skin Packs use vacuum to tightly enclose the product between a thin film and a paperboard. Each type of packaging offers unique benefits such as visibility, protection, and extended shelf life.</p></p>
