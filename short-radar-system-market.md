<p><strong>The Short Radar System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CW Radars</li><li>Pulsed Radars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Short Radar Systems, also known as radar detectors, are used to detect and measure the distance of target objects. CW Radars (Continuous Wave Radars) emit radio frequency signals continuously and measure the frequency shift caused by the moving target. They are commonly used in applications like collision avoidance systems. Pulsed Radars emit short bursts of radio frequency signals and measure the time it takes for the signal to bounce back from the target. They are utilized in radar guns for speed measurement and in air traffic control systems.</p></p>
