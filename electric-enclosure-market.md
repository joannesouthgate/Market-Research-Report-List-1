<p><ul><li>Metallic</li><li>Non-Metallic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric enclosure market comprises two main types: metallic and non-metallic enclosures. Metallic enclosures are made of materials such as stainless steel or aluminum, which provide excellent durability and protection against harsh environments. They are commonly used in industrial settings where high levels of protection are required. On the other hand, non-metallic enclosures are made of materials like polycarbonate or fiberglass, which offer lighter weight and corrosion resistance. They are suitable for outdoor or corrosive environments, where electrical components need protection without the added weight and potential for corrosion of metallic enclosures.</p></p>
