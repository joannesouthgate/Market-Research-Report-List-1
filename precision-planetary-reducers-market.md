<p><p>In the Precision Planetary Reducers market, there are two main types: Right Angle Precision Planetary Reducers and Parallel Output Shaft Precision Planetary Reducers. </p><p>Right Angle Precision Planetary Reducers are characterized by their ability to transmit torque at a 90-degree angle, making them suitable for applications where space is limited or when a change in direction is needed. </p><p>Parallel Output Shaft Precision Planetary Reducers, on the other hand, have an output shaft that is parallel to the input shaft. They are often used in applications where a linear movement is required and are known for their high efficiency and torque transmission capabilities.</p></p>
