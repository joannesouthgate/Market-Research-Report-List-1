Diaphragm metering pumps are commonly used due to their ability to deliver a consistent flow rate and handle a wide range of fluids. They use a flexible diaphragm to pump the fluids. Plunger metering pumps offer higher pressure capabilities and are used for applications that require precise and controlled fluid delivery. They use a reciprocating plunger to displace the fluid. Both types of pumps play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and reliable fluid metering in various industries.</p></p>
