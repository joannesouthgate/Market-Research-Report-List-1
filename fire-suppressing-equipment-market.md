<p><p>The fire suppressing equipment market comprises various types of devices used to prevent and extinguish fires. Fire extinguishers are portable devices that release substances to suppress flames. Fire sprinklers are fixed systems that dispense water or mist/fog to control and extinguish fires. Gas delivery systems use gases such as carbon dioxide or halons to suppress fires. Chemical and foam delivery systems release chemicals or foams to smother fires. Other types of fire suppressing equipment include fire blankets and fire retardant coatings, which provide additional protection against flames and heat.</p></p>
