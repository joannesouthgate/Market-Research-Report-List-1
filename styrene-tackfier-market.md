<p><p>Styrene tackifiers are used in various industries such as adhesives, coatings, and sealants to improve the tackiness of the products. There are two main types of styrene tackifiers: synthesis tackifiers and natural tackifiers. Synthesis tackifiers are chemically synthesized compounds that are derived from petroleum or other raw materials. On the other hand, natural tackifiers are derived from natural sources such as rosin or terpenes. Both types of tackifiers provide adhesive properties, but synthesis tackifiers offer better flexibility and stability, while natural tackifiers are more environmentally friendly.</p></p>
