<p><h1>Gearless Traction Machine Market Analysis and Market Size: Global Industry Overview, Market Segmentation and Forecast (2024 to 2031)</h1></p><p><strong>Gearless Traction Machine Market Analysis and Latest Trends</strong></p>
<p><p>A gearless traction machine is an electromechanical device used in elevators to provide vertical transportation. It operates without the need for traditional gears, making it more efficient, reliable, and quiet compared to geared machines. Gearless traction machines consist of a rotating sheave or drum that is directly connected to the elevator's cables. These machines are usually driven by a three-phase AC synchronous motor, which offers high performance and precision.</p><p>The gearless traction machine market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for energy-efficient elevator systems. Gearless traction machines offer higher energy efficiency compared to geared machines, enabling significant cost savings for building owners and operators. Additionally, the growing emphasis on green building initiatives and sustainable transportation is boosting the adoption of gearless traction machines.</p><p>The market is also driven by the rising urbanization and increasing construction activities worldwide. The need for efficient vertical transportation in high-rise buildings, airports, shopping malls, and other commercial and residential complexes is propelling the demand for gearless traction machines.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology have led to the development of compact gearless traction machines that require less space, making them suitable for installation in both new and existing buildings. These machines also offer improved safety features and smoother ride quality, contributing to their market growth.</p><p>Overall, the gearless traction machine market is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years, driven by factors such as energy efficiency, urbanization, and technological advancements in the elevator industry. The market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period.</p></p>
