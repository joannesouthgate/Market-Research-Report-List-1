<p><p>Digital aerial cameras are advanced camera systems used to capture high-resolution aerial images for various applications such as remote sensing, surveying, mapping, and surveillance. The market is expected to witness steady growth due to the rising adoption of unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) for aerial photography and mapping applications.
