<p><p>The wafer bonder market is categorized into two types: semi-automated wafer bonder and automated wafer bonder. Semi-automated wafer bonder refers to a bonding process that involves some manual intervention by the operator. It requires human input for certain steps such as loading/unloading wafers or aligning them. In contrast, automated wafer bonder is a more advanced technology that minimizes human involvement. It executes the bonding process automatically, including alignment and loading/unloading of wafers, resulting in improved efficiency and reduced human error.</p></p>
