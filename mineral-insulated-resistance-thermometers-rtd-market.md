<p><p>Mineral Insulated Resistance Thermometers (RTD) are sensors used to measure temperature in various industries. The market types for RTD include Simplex Conductor and Duplex Conductor. Simplex Conductor RTDs consist of a single conductor, typically made of platinum, while Duplex Conductor RTDs have two parallel conductors. Simplex Conductor RTDs are more affordable and commonly used in applications where accuracy is not critical. Duplex Conductor RTDs, on the other hand, offer higher accuracy and are used in industries like healthcare and scientific research where precision is important.</p></p>
