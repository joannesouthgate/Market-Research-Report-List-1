<p><p>There are different types of ice creams and frozen desserts available in the market, such as soft ice cream and hard ice cream. Soft ice cream is made by incorporating air during the freezing process, resulting in a light and creamy texture. It is served directly from a soft-serve machine. On the other hand, hard ice cream has a denser texture and is typically stored and served at colder temperatures. It is made through a slower churn and freeze process, resulting in a firm and scoopable consistency. Both types offer a variety of flavors and toppings to cater to different preferences.</p></p>
