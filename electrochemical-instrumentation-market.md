<p><p>Electrochemical Instrumentation refers to the market for devices used to measure and analyze electrochemical processes. Potentiostats/Galvanostats measure and control electrical potential/current during electrochemical reactions. Ion Chromatographs determine the concentration of ions in a sample. Conductivity Meters gauge the ability of a solution to conduct electricity. Salinity Meters measure the salt content of water. Titrators determine the concentration of an unknown substance through titration. pH Meters measure the acidity or alkalinity of a solution. Lastly, "Others" includes various instruments like coulometers and electrochemical sensors used in specific applications.</p></p>
