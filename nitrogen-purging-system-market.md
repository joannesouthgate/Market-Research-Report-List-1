<p><strong>The Nitrogen Purging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dilution Nitrogen Purging</li><li>Displacement Nitrogen Purging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nitrogen purging systems are used to eliminate the presence of oxygen and other contaminants in various industries. Dilution nitrogen purging involves the gradual replacement of air with nitrogen, reducing the oxygen concentration to a safe level. This method is commonly used in applications where a small amount of oxygen is permissible. Displacement nitrogen purging, on the other hand, completely replaces the oxygen with nitrogen, ensuring an oxygen-free environment. This method is typically employed in situations where any traces of oxygen could cause combustion or corrosion hazards.</p></p>
