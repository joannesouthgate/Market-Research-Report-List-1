<p><p>Street light controllers are used to manage and control the operation of street lights. Analog-type controllers utilize analog technology, where electrical signals are continuously variable. They offer basic control functions and have limited features. Digital-type controllers, on the other hand, use digital technology to provide advanced features such as programmable scheduling, remote control, and energy management. They offer higher accuracy, versatility, and adaptability. Digital controllers are more advanced compared to analog controllers and are increasingly being adopted in modern street lighting systems.</p></p>
