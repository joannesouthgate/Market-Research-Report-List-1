<p><p>Crane limit switches are crucial safety components in the operation of cranes. Inching switches are designed to control small movements of cranes, facilitating precise positioning and reducing the chances of accidents. They act as a safety mechanism by stopping the crane's movement when activated. Speed switches, on the other hand, monitor and regulate the speed of crane operations. They ensure that cranes are operating within safe speed limits, preventing over-speeding and potential accidents. Both types of limit switches play a vital role in ensuring the smooth and safe functioning of cranes in various industries.</p></p>
