<p><p>Molten Salt Thermal Energy Storage (TES) is a technology used in solar power systems to store and release heat energy for electricity generation. There are two main types of systems utilizing this technology: Tower-type Solar Power Tower System and Dish Concentrating Solar Power Systems. The Tower-type system uses a central tower to collect and store energy, while the Dish system uses multiple mirrors to concentrate sunlight onto a receiver. Both systems use molten salt as a medium to store thermal energy for power generation.</p></p>
