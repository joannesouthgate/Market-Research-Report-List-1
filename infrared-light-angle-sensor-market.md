<p><p>The Infrared Light Angle Sensor market is categorized into various types based on the wavelength of infrared light. These types include Short Wave IR (SWIR), Mid Wave IR (MWIR), Long Wave IR (LWIR), and Far Wave IR (FWIR). SWIR sensors detect wavelengths between 0.9 to 1.7 micrometers, MWIR sensors detect wavelengths between 3 to 5 micrometers, LWIR sensors detect wavelengths between 8 to 14 micrometers, and FWIR sensors detect wavelengths beyond 14 micrometers. Each type of sensor is designed for specific applications and industries, ranging from surveillance and automotive to medical and aerospace.</p></p>
