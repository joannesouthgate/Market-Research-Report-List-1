<p><strong>The Differential Refractometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Refraction Type</li><li>Reflective Type</li><li>Interference Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Differential refractometers are used to measure the refractive index of a material, which is a key property in various industries. Refraction type refractometers work by measuring the bending of light as it passes through a sample. Reflective type refractometers use the reflection of light to determine the refractive index. Interference type refractometers utilize the interference pattern between two beams of light to make measurements. Each type has its own advantages and applications, making differential refractometers a versatile tool for quality control and research purposes.</p></p>
