<p><p>The Military Aerospace Landing Gear Market consists of two main types: Main Landing Gear and Nose/Tail Landing Gear. Main Landing Gear supports the majority of the aircraft's weight during takeoff and landing and typically consists of two or more wheels on each side. Nose/Tail Landing Gear, on the other hand, is located at the front or rear of the aircraft and is responsible for steering during taxiing and providing stability during ground operations. Both types play a critical role in ensuring safe and efficient aircraft operations in military aerospace applications.</p></p>
