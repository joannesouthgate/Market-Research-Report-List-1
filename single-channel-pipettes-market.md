<p><p>Single channel pipettes are laboratory instruments used for accurately measuring and transferring small volumes of liquids. These pipettes typically have a single nozzle and are capable of dispensing volume ranges from microliters to milliliters. They are widely used in various applications such as biological research, pharmaceuticals, diagnostics, and clinical testing.</p><p>The single channel pipettes market is experiencing significant growth due to several factors. Firstly, the increasing demand for accurate and precise liquid handling in the healthcare and pharmaceutical sectors is driving the market growth. Single channel pipettes offer superior accuracy and ease of use, making them ideal for such applications. Additionally, the rising investments in research and development activities and advances in technology have further fueled market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the COVID-19 pandemic has also positively impacted the single channel pipettes market. The need for large-scale testing and research related to the virus has created a surge in demand for these instruments. Furthermore, ongoing technological advancements, such as the integration of electronic features and ergonomic designs, are driving the adoption of single channel pipettes.</p><p>In terms of market trends, there is a growing preference for electronic single channel pipettes over traditional manual ones. Electronic pipettes offer advanced features like programmable settings, multiple dispensing modes, and improved ergonomics, enhancing efficiency and productivity in laboratories. Additionally, there is a rising trend of miniaturization in the healthcare and pharmaceutical industries, driving the adoption of single channel pipettes with smaller volume ranges.</p><p>Overall, the single channel pipettes market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period, with a projected CAGR of 8.7%.
