<p><p>A capacitive touchscreen is a type of display technology that uses the electrical properties of the human body to detect touch inputs. Capacitive touchscreens are widely used in various electronic devices, including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and gaming consoles.</p><p>The future outlook for the capacitive touchscreen market is highly promising. The increasing adoption of touch-based devices across various industries, such as consumer electronics, healthcare, automotive, and retail, is fueling the demand for capacitive touchscreens.
