<p><p>The automotive grade inductors market consists of two major types: SMD power inductors and plug-in power inductors. SMD power inductors are surface-mounted components that provide efficient power conversion and noise suppression in automotive applications. They offer high current capability and are compact in size. On the other hand, plug-in power inductors are through-hole components that offer high energy efficiency and are suitable for rugged automotive environments. Both types of inductors play a crucial role in the functioning of automotive systems by storing and releasing energy in the form of magnetic fields.</p></p>
