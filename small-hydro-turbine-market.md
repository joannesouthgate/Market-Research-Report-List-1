<p><p>The Small Hydro Turbine market consists of various types, namely Francis, Kaplan, Pelton, and Others. The Francis turbine is popular for its versatility and ability to handle a wide range of water flow rates, making it suitable for medium heads. The Kaplan turbine is known for its adjustable blades, making it efficient for low to medium head applications. The Pelton turbine is specifically designed for high heads and low flow rates, utilizing multiple cups to efficiently convert water energy. Other types include cross-flow and tubular turbines, which cater to specific project requirements.</p></p>
