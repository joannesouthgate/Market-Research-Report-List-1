HDPE machines utilize high-density polyethylene, which is known for its strength and durability. PET machines specialize in molding products using polyethylene terephthalate, a material commonly used for packaging and containers. "Others" refers to blow molding machines that work with different types of materials apart from LDPE, HDPE, and PET.</p></p>
