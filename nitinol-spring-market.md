<p><p>The Nitinol Spring market comprises three main types: compression springs, extension springs, and torsion springs. Compression springs are designed to resist compression and store potential energy when compressed. Extension springs, on the other hand, are used to resist stretching and store energy when extended. Torsion springs, the third type, store energy by resisting twisting or rotational forces. These springs are primarily used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and healthcare, where their unique properties and shape memory characteristics of Nitinol are highly advantageous.</p></p>
