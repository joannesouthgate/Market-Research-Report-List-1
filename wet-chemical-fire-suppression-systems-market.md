<p><strong>The Wet Chemical Fire Suppression Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Release System</li><li>Indirect Release System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wet Chemical Fire Suppression Systems are designed to extinguish fires by suppressing the flames and cooling the surrounding area. Direct Release systems involve the discharge of the agent at the source of the fire, providing immediate suppression. On the other hand, Indirect Release systems involve the discharge of the agent into the protected area, creating a barrier between the fire and other areas. Both systems are effective in suppressing different types of fires, offering protection in various industries like commercial kitchens, manufacturing plants, and data centers.</p></p>
