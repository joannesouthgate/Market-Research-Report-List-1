<p><p>The UV LED market consists of three main types: UV-A LED, UV-B LED, and UV-C LED. UV-A LEDs emit ultraviolet light with a wavelength range of 315 to 400 nanometers. These LEDs are commonly used for applications like curing, counterfeit detection, and bug zappers. UV-B LEDs emit ultraviolet light with a wavelength range of 280 to 315 nanometers and find use in medical treatments, plant growth stimulation, and UV sterilization. UV-C LEDs emit ultraviolet light with a wavelength range of 100 to 280 nanometers and are primarily used for disinfection and purification purposes.</p></p>
