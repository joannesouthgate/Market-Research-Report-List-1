<p><p>High Performance Computing (HPC) market is categorized into two types: On-Premises and Cloud market. It is suited for sensitive data and long-term intensive projects. On the other hand, Cloud HPC involves utilizing third-party cloud service providers, offering scalability, flexibility, and pay-as-you-go models. It is suitable for short-term or variable workloads, enabling access to a vast amount of computing power without the need for extensive upfront investment.</p></p>
