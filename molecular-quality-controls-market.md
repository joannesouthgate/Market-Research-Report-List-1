<p><strong>The Molecular Quality Controls Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Independent</li><li>Instrument Specific</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the molecular quality controls market, there are two types: independent controls and instrument-specific controls. Independent controls are designed to work with multiple instruments from different manufacturers, providing a standardized quality assessment. These controls offer flexibility and compatibility, allowing laboratories to use them with various instruments. On the other hand, instrument-specific controls are tailored to work with a specific instrument or platform. These controls ensure accuracy and precision for a specific system, catering to the unique requirements of that instrument.</p></p>
