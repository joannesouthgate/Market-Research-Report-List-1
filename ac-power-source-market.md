<p><strong>The AC Power Source Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear AC Power Sources</li><li>PWM AC Power Sources</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC power sources are used to provide electrical power for various devices and equipment. Two common types of AC power sources are linear AC power sources and PWM AC power sources. </p><p>Linear AC power sources use linear regulators to directly convert the input voltage to the desired output voltage. They provide a clean and stable power output but are less efficient and bulkier compared to other types.</p><p>PWM AC power sources use pulse-width modulation techniques to vary the output voltage. They are more efficient and compact but can introduce some levels of distortion in the power output. </p><p>Overall, the choice between linear and PWM AC power sources depends on specific requirements and trade-offs in terms of power quality, efficiency, and size.</p></p>
