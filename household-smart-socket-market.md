<p><p>A household smart socket, also known as a smart plug, is an advanced electrical socket that enables users to control the power supply of devices remotely. These smart sockets are equipped with Wi-Fi connectivity, allowing users to remotely turn on/off devices, set timers, monitor energy usage, and control multiple devices simultaneously.</p><p>The household smart socket market has been experiencing significant growth over the past few years and is projected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Consumers are seeking convenient and efficient ways to manage their household appliances, contributing to the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the rising focus on energy efficiency and the need to reduce electricity consumption are also propelling the market's expansion. Household smart sockets allow users to monitor their energy usage, schedule power on/off timings, and eliminate standby or vampire power, leading to considerable energy savings.</p><p>Additionally, the integration of artificial intelligence and the Internet of Things (IoT) is revolutionizing the household smart socket market. With AI-powered capabilities, these smart sockets can learn users' preferences and independently adjust the power supply to optimize energy efficiency.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing the emergence of innovative features such as surge protection, USB charging ports, and compatibility with smart speakers, enhancing the overall functionality and appeal of smart sockets.</p><p>Overall, the continuous advancements in smart home technology, increasing consumer awareness about energy management, and the convenience offered by household smart sockets are driving the market's growth and are expected to fuel its expansion in the coming years.</p></p>
