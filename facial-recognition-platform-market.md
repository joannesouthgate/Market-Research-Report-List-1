<p><p>Facial recognition technology has various market types. Lastly, facial analytics market encompasses the analysis of facial expressions, emotions, and demographic information, enabling applications in areas like marketing and security. These market types collectively contribute to the development and implementation of facial recognition platforms.</p></p>
