<p><p>Laser Doppler Velocimetry (LDV) is a measurement technique used to determine the velocity of small particles or flow within a fluid. There are two types of LDV market receivers: Forward Scatter Receiver and Backward Scatter Receiver. The Forward Scatter Receiver detects the scattered light moving at different angles from the direction of the incident laser beam. In contrast, the Backward Scatter Receiver detects the light scattered directly opposite to the incident laser beam. Both receivers are crucial in LDV systems for accurate and comprehensive velocity measurements.</p></p>
