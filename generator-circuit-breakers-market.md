<p><strong>The Generator Circuit Breakers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Blast Circuit Breakers</li><li>Vacuum Circuit Breakers</li><li>SF6 Circuit Breakers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Generator circuit breakers are essential devices for protecting power generators from electrical faults. There are three main types of generator circuit breakers - Air Blast Circuit Breakers, Vacuum Circuit Breakers, and SF6 Circuit Breakers.</p><p>Air Blast Circuit Breakers use compressed air to extinguish the arc, while Vacuum Circuit Breakers use a vacuum to isolate the contacts. SF6 Circuit Breakers use sulfur hexafluoride gas to quench the arc. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different applications based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
