<p><p>Gas turbine service market can be categorized into two main types: heavy-duty services and aero-derivative services. These services are designed to ensure optimal performance, reliability, and longevity of these gas turbines. On the other hand, aero-derivative services specialize in the maintenance, repair, and overhaul of smaller gas turbines typically used in aviation and mobile power applications. These services aim to ensure safe and efficient operation of these turbines.</p></p>
