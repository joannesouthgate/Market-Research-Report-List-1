<p><p>Humidity sensors are devices used to measure the amount of moisture present in the air. There are two main types of humidity sensors: relative humidity sensors and absolute humidity sensors. Relative humidity sensors measure the moisture content in the air relative to the maximum amount of moisture the air can hold at a given temperature. Absolute humidity sensors, on the other hand, measure the actual amount of moisture in the air without any reference to temperature. Both types of sensors are used in various industries such as meteorology, agriculture, HVAC systems, and manufacturing.</p></p>
