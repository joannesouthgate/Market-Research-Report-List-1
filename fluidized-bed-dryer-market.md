<p><p>The fluidized-bed dryer market consists of two types: single layer and multi-layer dryers. Single layer dryers are designed with a single drying chamber, allowing for efficient and cost-effective drying processes. They are suitable for applications with limited space and lower production volumes. On the other hand, multi-layer dryers feature multiple drying chambers stacked one above the other, enabling a higher drying capacity and increased productivity. These dryers are ideal for applications with larger production volumes and where space is not a constraint.</p></p>
