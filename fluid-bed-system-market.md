<p><p>A fluid bed system, also known as a fluidized bed, is a process used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemical, food, and agriculture, among others. This system offers advantages such as uniform drying, efficient mixing, and controlled granulation and coating processes.</p><p>The fluid bed system market has experienced significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 11.7% during the forecast period. Additionally, the growing trend of personalized medicine and the increasing need for cost-effective manufacturing processes are driving the demand for fluid bed systems in the pharmaceutical sector.</p><p>In the food industry, fluid bed systems are used for processes such as drying, cooling, and coating of food products.
