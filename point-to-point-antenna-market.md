<p><p>The point-to-point antenna market comprises various types of antennas that enable wireless communication between two specific points. Parabolic antennas use a concave dish to focus radio waves, providing a long-range and high-gain communication link. Flat panel antennas are compact and offer a broader coverage area, making them suitable for urban environments. Yagi antennas are highly directional with long elements that enhance signal concentration and transmission distance. Each antenna type offers unique features and benefits, catering to specific communication requirements in various settings.</p></p>
