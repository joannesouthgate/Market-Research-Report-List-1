<p><p>The electric clothes dryer market is comprised of two main types: vented electric clothes dryers and non-vented electric clothes dryers. Vented electric clothes dryers are designed to expel hot, moist air by using a venting system, which requires proper ventilation through an external duct or wall. On the other hand, non-vented electric clothes dryers do not require any external venting as they use condensation or heat pump technologies to remove moisture from the clothes, converting it into water that can be collected in a reservoir or discharged through a drain.</p></p>
