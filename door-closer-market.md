<p><ul><li>Surface Applied Door Closer</li><li>Concealed Door Closer</li><li>Floor Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Door Closer market is categorized into three types, each serving a specific purpose. The Surface Applied Door Closer is installed on the surface of the door and is visible to the user. It offers easy installation and adjustment options. The Concealed Door Closer is installed within the door frame, making it invisible to the user. It provides a seamless aesthetic appearance without compromising functionality. Finally, the Floor Spring market offers concealed installation on the floor, allowing the door to swing smoothly. These variations cater to different architectural and design requirements, providing diverse options for users.</p></p>
