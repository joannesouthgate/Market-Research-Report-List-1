<p><p>The protective medical gowns market is composed of two main types: disposable surgical gowns and reusable surgical gowns. Disposable surgical gowns are designed for single-use and are made from a lightweight, non-woven fabric that provides a barrier against fluids and microorganisms. They are often preferred in healthcare settings as they eliminate the need for laundering and sterilization. On the other hand, reusable surgical gowns are made from a heavier, more durable fabric that can withstand repeated cycles of washing and sterilization. They are primarily used in facilities where there is a need for long-term use and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
