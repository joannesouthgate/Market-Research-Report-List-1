<p><strong>The Automotive Mono Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive mono camera market can be segmented into two types: CCD (charge-coupled device) and CMOS (complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor). CCD cameras capture high-quality images with low noise levels and are commonly used in professional photography. On the other hand, CMOS cameras are more cost-effective and energy-efficient, making them popular for automotive applications. Both types of cameras play a crucial role in enabling advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and autonomous driving technologies by providing visual data to the onboard systems for various purposes like lane departure warning, collision detection, and object recognition.</p></p>
