<p><ul><li>Optical</li><li>Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shaft Measuring Machines are used to measure and inspect shafts with precision. Optical machines utilize optical sensors and cameras to capture images of the shaft and measure various parameters like diameter, roundness, and straightness. These machines are ideal for non-contact measurement and offer high accuracy. Mechanical machines, on the other hand, employ physical contact with the shaft using probes and gauges to measure its dimensions. They are more suitable for rough measurements and can handle larger shafts. Each type of machine has its own advantages and applications in the Shaft Measuring Machine market.</p></p>
