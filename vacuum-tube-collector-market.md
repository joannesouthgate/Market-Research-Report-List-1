<p><p>A vacuum tube collector, also known as a solar tube collector, is a type of solar thermal collector that uses glass tubes containing a heat-absorbing coating. These tubes are connected to a manifold where the absorbed heat is transferred to a fluid (usually water or a mixture of water and antifreeze), which is then used for heating purposes or to produce hot water.</p><p>The market for vacuum tube collectors has been witnessing significant growth due to the increasing adoption of renewable energy sources and the rising demand for efficient heating systems.
