<p><p>The Automotive H Bridge Driver market includes two main types: Single H-Bridge and Dual H-Bridge. The Single H-Bridge driver is designed to control the direction and speed of one motor, commonly used in applications where only one motor needs to be controlled, such as power windows or wipers. In contrast, the Dual H-Bridge driver is capable of controlling two motors independently or a single motor with increased power requirements, commonly used in applications like robotic vehicles or electric scooters that require more complex motor control.</p></p>
