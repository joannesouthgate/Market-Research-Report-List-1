<p><ul><li>Analog Meters</li><li>Digital Meters</li><li>Smart Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power metering market refers to the industry involving the measurement of electricity consumption. Analog meters are traditional meters that use rotating dials to measure energy usage. Digital meters provide numerical readings and are more accurate and efficient than analog meters. Smart meters, on the other hand, are advanced digital meters that enable two-way communication between utilities and consumers, allowing for real-time monitoring, remote reading, and automated billing. Smart meters also offer features like load monitoring, outage detection, and energy management, enabling efficient energy consumption and cost savings.</p></p>
