<p><p>The autonomous vehicle sensors market can be divided into three key types: camera sensors, radar sensors, and lidar sensors. Camera sensors use optical imaging technology to capture visual data and identify objects and road signs. Radar sensors rely on radio waves to detect and estimate the distance of objects, allowing for collision avoidance. Lidar sensors employ laser beams to create 3D maps of the surrounding environment, offering precise measurements of distance and detecting obstacles. These sensor technologies are critical for enabling autonomous vehicles to perceive their surroundings and navigate safely.</p></p>
