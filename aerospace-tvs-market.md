<p><p>The aerospace TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressor) market consists of two main types: Uni-polar TVS and Bi-polar TVS. Uni-polar TVS devices are designed to protect against overvoltage events in one direction only, typically used in circuits where current flows primarily in one direction. On the other hand, Bi-polar TVS devices can safeguard against overvoltage events in both directions, making them suitable for circuits with alternating currents or bidirectional flow. Both types ensure the protection of sensitive aerospace electronics from voltage spikes, ensuring their reliability and performance.</p></p>
