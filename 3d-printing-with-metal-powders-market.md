<p><strong>The 3D Printing with Metal Powders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DMLS</li><li>SLM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D printing with metal powders market consists of two major types: Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS) and Selective Laser Melting (SLM). DMLS involves selectively melting layers of metal powders together using a high-power laser to create a solid object. This process is commonly used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and medical. On the other hand, SLM involves fully melting the metal powders with a high-power laser to achieve a stronger bond between layers. This technique is often preferred for producing complex metal parts with high precision and mechanical properties.</p></p>
