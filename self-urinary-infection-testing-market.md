<p><p>The self-urinary infection testing market offers different types of tests for detecting urinary infections at home. Dipsticks are strips that can be dipped in urine and provide quick results by changing color. Urine Assay Kits are comprehensive tests that analyze multiple components in urine, such as pH levels and specific gravity, to determine infection. Creatinine Urine Diagnostic Test Kits measure creatinine levels to assess kidney function. Home Protein Urine Test Strips detect the presence of protein, which can indicate infection. These tests enable individuals to monitor their urinary health conveniently at home.</p></p>
