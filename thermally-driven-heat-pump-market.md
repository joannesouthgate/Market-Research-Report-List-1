<p><strong>The Thermally Driven Heat Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Closed Loop</li><li>Horizontal Closed Loop</li><li>Open Loop</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermally driven heat pump market consists of various types, including vertical closed loop, horizontal closed loop, open loop, and other markets. In the vertical closed loop system, a series of holes are drilled vertically into the ground, allowing heat transfer with the help of a heat pump. The horizontal closed loop system uses horizontal piping buried underground for heat exchange. An open loop system utilizes a water source, such as a well or lake, for heat transfer. Lastly, the other market includes unique variations or hybrid combinations of these systems, providing alternative options for thermally driven heat pump setups.</p></p>
