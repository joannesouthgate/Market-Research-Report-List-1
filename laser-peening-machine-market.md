<p><p>A Laser Peening Machine is a high-tech tool used in the process of laser peening. Laser peening is a surface enhancement technique that improves the fatigue life and strength of materials by inducing compressive residual stresses into the target material. This process involves directing a high-energy laser beam onto a target surface, which creates a shock wave that helps modify its properties.</p><p>The global Laser Peening Machine Market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. The market is driven by the increasing demand for advanced surface enhancement techniques in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and power generation.
