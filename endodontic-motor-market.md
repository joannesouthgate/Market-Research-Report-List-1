<p><p>The endodontic motor market consists of two main types: programmable and not programmable. Programmable motors provide the flexibility to set different parameters such as torque and speed for particular treatments, offering customization according to specific patient needs. On the other hand, not programmable motors offer a fixed set of parameters, meaning that they're designed with pre-determined torque and speed settings for general use. Overall, programmable motors enable more personalized treatment options, while not programmable motors provide a standardized approach for various endodontic procedures.</p></p>
