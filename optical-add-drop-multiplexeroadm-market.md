Two main types of OADM markets are Fixed Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer and Reconfigurable Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer. Fixed OADMs have a fixed configuration and are limited in flexibility, while Reconfigurable OADMs offer more flexibility and can be adjusted to add or drop different wavelengths of light signals as needed. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing network performance and managing signal traffic efficiently.</p></p>
