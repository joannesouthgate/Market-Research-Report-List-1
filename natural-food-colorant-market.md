Lutein, a yellow pigment, is derived from marigold flowers and is used in the coloring of dairy products and baked goods. Capsanthin, an orange-red pigment extracted from red peppers, is utilized for coloring processed foods. Anthocyanins, found in fruits and vegetables, are used to provide red, blue, and purple hues to food products. Other natural colorants include turmeric, beet powder, and spirulina.</p></p>
