<p><p>Fire alarm detection systems are important for early detection of fire and timely evacuation. The market offers three main types of fire alarm detection systems: photoelectric, ionization, and dual sensor. Photoelectric systems use a light source to detect smoke particles, while ionization systems use ionized particles to identify smoke. Dual sensor systems combine both photoelectric and ionization technologies to enhance overall fire detection accuracy. Each system has its strengths and weaknesses, and selection depends on specific requirements such as the environment and potential fire risks.</p></p>
