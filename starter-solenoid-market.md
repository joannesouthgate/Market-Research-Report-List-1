<p><p>A starter solenoid is an electromechanical device responsible for starting an internal combustion engine. It is a relay switch that controls the flow of electrical current from the battery to the starter motor, which in turn helps to being the engine to life. The solenoid contains a coil of wire wrapped around an iron core. When current flows through the coil, it creates a magnetic field that pulls a plunger or a switch, connecting the battery to the starter motor.</p><p>The future outlook of the starter solenoid market looks promising.
