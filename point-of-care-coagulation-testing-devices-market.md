<p><p>Point-of-Care Coagulation Testing Devices market includes three main types: instruments/analyzers, consumables, and accessories. Instruments or analyzers are the main devices used for coagulation testing, which measure the clotting time of blood samples. Consumables refer to the disposable items needed for the testing process, such as test strips or reagents. Accessories include additional equipment or components that enhance the functionality of the devices, like control materials or calibration tools. These three components are essential for performing accurate and efficient coagulation tests at the point-of-care.</p></p>
