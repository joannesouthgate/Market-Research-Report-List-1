<p><p>The EV high voltage cables market consists of two types: shielded cables and unshielded cables. Shielded cables are designed with a protective layer to minimize electromagnetic interference and provide better signal quality. They are commonly used in applications where noise reduction is crucial, such as in industrial machinery. On the other hand, unshielded cables do not have this protective layer and are suitable for applications where interference is not a significant concern, such as in residential wiring.</p></p>
