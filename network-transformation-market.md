Firstly, the rapid proliferation of digitalization and cloud services is driving the need for reliable, scalable, and secure networks. Additionally, the rising popularity of 5G networks and the Internet of Things (IoT) is further boosting market growth as network transformation is essential for supporting these technologies.</p><p>Moreover, the COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated the network transformation market growth.
