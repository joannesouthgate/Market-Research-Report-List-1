<p><p>Portable propane water heaters come in two main types: with tank and tankless. With tank water heaters store and heat a specific amount of water in a tank, allowing for a continuous supply of hot water until the tank is depleted. Tankless water heaters, on the other hand, heat water on-demand without the need for a storage tank, providing an endless supply of hot water. Both types of heaters are fueled by propane and offer the convenience of portability, making them ideal for outdoor activities or for use in areas without access to electricity or natural gas.</p></p>
