<p><strong>The Commercial Steer Axle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Driven Steer Axles</li><li>Non-Driven Steer Axles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial steer axles can be classified into two types: driven steer axles and non-driven steer axles. Driven steer axles are connected to the vehicle's drivetrain and are responsible for transferring power from the engine to the wheels, enabling the vehicle to move forward. On the other hand, non-driven steer axles are not connected to the drivetrain and are solely responsible for steering the vehicle. These two types of steer axles play distinct roles in the functioning and maneuverability of commercial vehicles.</p></p>
