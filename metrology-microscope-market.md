<p><p>The metrology microscope market is predominantly divided into three types: optical microscope, electron microscope, and others. Optical microscopes are widely used and utilize visible light to magnify and view specimens. They are suitable for several applications in research, education, and industrial sectors. Electron microscopes, on the other hand, use a focused beam of electrons to provide higher resolution and magnification capabilities, assisting in nanoscale analysis. The "others" category includes various types of metrology microscopes, such as scanning probe microscopes, fluorescence microscopes, and confocal microscopes, each with unique features and applications.</p></p>
