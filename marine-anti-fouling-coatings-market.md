<p><strong>The Marine Anti-Fouling Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self Polishing Copolymer (SPC) Coating</li><li>Dirt Release Coating (FRC)</li><li>Ablative Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine anti-fouling coatings are used to protect ship hulls from fouling organisms to improve fuel efficiency and reduce maintenance costs. Self Polishing Copolymer (SPC) Coatings continuously release biocides as the polymer wears, providing long-lasting protection. Dirt Release Coatings (FRC) prevent organisms from settling by creating a smooth surface that can be easily cleaned. Ablative coatings slowly erode over time, releasing biocides to deter fouling. Each type offers unique benefits and are used based on specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
