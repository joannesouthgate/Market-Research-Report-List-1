<p><p>The electronic prototyping market consists of two main types: PCB prototyping and electronic product prototyping. PCB prototyping refers to the creation of a prototype circuit board, which is an essential component of electronic devices. It involves designing and manufacturing a small batch of circuit boards for testing and evaluation purposes. On the other hand, electronic product prototyping involves developing a prototype of an entire electronic product, including its circuits, components, and external structure. This type of prototyping helps in validating the product's design and functionality before mass production.</p></p>
