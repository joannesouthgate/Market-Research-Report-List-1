<p><p>The near infrared (NIR) spectrometer market is classified into several types: FT-NIR (Fourier Transform-NIR), UV-Vis-NIR (Ultraviolet-Visible-NIR), Raman, and Others. FT-NIR spectrometers utilize an interferometer to obtain NIR spectra, allowing for high-speed analysis with good sensitivity and accuracy. UV-Vis-NIR spectrometers cover a wider range of wavelengths and are useful for a broader range of applications. Raman spectrometers employ lasers to interact with molecules and provide detailed chemical information. Other types include dispersive NIR spectrometers and handheld/portable NIR analyzers. Each type offers unique features and benefits for various analytical purposes.</p></p>
